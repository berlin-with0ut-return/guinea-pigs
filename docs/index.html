Hi! This is a site for my two guinea pigs, Mufasa and Donuts. Mufasa is a mixed-breed male guinea pig born in 2016 (3 years old as of 2019). He has long white hair with black, tan, and brown spots. He was rescued from a shelter, so he's a little shy towards humans, but is super affectionate after you get to know him! His favorite food is sweet pepper. Donuts is an American short-hair female guinea pig born in 2015 (4 years old as of 2019). She is an albino with red eyes and white-grey fur. Unlike Mufasa, Donuts is super energetic and loves running, jumping, and climbing just about anywhere. Her favorite food is baby carrots. Look around to learn more about them!
